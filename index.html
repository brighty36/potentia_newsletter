This quarter’s briefing

A note from Suzy Hassan

“The problem is getting worse — and we built something to address it.”

We know that XX% of online survey entrants are fraudulent. Bots, duplicates, straight-liners, AI-generated open ends. They are all finding their way into research data, and the problem is getting worse.

Our clients are making huge decisions off the back of online data collection. You need confidence in the data you receive. We took that seriously, and we built something to address it.

Data Quality

Potentia Protect

We are panel agnostic and picky. Pooling together the best sample sources and acting as your filter to deliver high quality data. There is no silver bullet, so we built a layered defence.

Verisoul Integration
